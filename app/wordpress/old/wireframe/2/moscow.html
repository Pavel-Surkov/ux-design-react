Detailed itinerary

3 days & 2 night

Day 1:

• Departure on view in "the Circus of dancing fountains Akvamarin" public transport.

• Meeting the group at the station.

• Breakfast at city cafe.

• Bus excursion "Christmas Moscow". Visit Red square, Moscow University, Cathedral of Christ

   the Savior, Moscow-city, the Vorobiovy hills.

• Bus excursion "Christmas Tree". The guide will acquaint you with the main fur-trees of Moscow.

• Lunch at city cafe. Transfer to the hotel. Accommodation.

• A bus for 6 PM

Day 2:

• A walk in the Park "Zaryadye".

• Breakfast at the hotel buffet. Meeting with the guide.

• Departure by public transport.

• Visit the Kremlin Christmas tree.

• Lunch at city cafe

• Excursion to the Kremlin with visit to the Cathedral, the Palace complex, the Arsenal, the Senate, the Tsar

   cannon and the Tsar bell. Tour "Ancient capital" in red square, visit the monument to Minin

   and Pozharsky, a place of execution, building the largest Department store in the country.

• Free time. Pedestrian day

Day 3:

• Transfer to the railway station. Free time.

• Breakfast at the hotel buffet. The release numbers. Meeting with the guide in the hotel lobby.

• Tour the Museum of Christmas toys factory in Sokolniki. Master class on painting Christmas ball.

• Lunch at city cafe.

• Bus tour "Moscow house". Izmailovo, Lefortovo, Lyublino, Kolomenskoe, Tsaritsyno.

• Bus excursion to the Museum-estate “Kolomenskoye”. Tour to the Palace of Alexei Mikhailovich

   "Eighth wonder of the world".

• The bus at 8 PM

sightseeing according to the program, entrance tickets, accommodation in hotel of selected category, 2 meals, transport on the tour, support group.
