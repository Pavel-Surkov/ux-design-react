Description

Spanish devourer of maidens ' hearts unveiled its next perfume masterpiece for ladies - Blue Seduction for Women. "Blue temptation" plunges us into the sun-drenched beaches, historic towns on the cliffs, where the wide palette of landscapes, full of beautiful places to walk near the sea.


The art of flirting, which is owned by every woman starts with a fun game of sweet dessert notes and a spicy Oriental tones and tart drops of raspberry liqueur.The fragrance is based on a water accord in which there is a mysterious "sugar note". Blue Seduction for Women starts with top notes of citrus, juicy watery fruits and violets that give way to the "heart" of shades of rose, Jasmine, raspberry and the "base" of the Eastern sweet chords.
